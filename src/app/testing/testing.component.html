Advanced Penetration Testing

Course Description

This advanced course focuses on mastering penetration testing techniques, from exploiting vulnerabilities in complex systems to bypassing modern defenses. Students will gain hands-on experience with industry-leading tools and strategies used by professional penetration testers.

Learning Outcomes

  • Master advanced techniques used in penetration testing
  • Gain in-depth knowledge of vulnerability scanning and exploitation
  • Learn to exploit complex systems and bypass security mechanisms
  • Prepare for certifications like CEH and OSCP

Prerequisites

Ethical Hacking Essentials, Network Security Basics

Duration & Schedule

  • Total Duration: 1 Month
  • Offline (Saturday & Sunday): 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (5 hours each day)
  • Online (Monday to Thursday): 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM (1 hour each day)
  • Total Course Hours: 40 hours (20 offline + 20 online)
