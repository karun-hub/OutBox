Introduction to Cybersecurity

Course Description

This course is designed for beginners to dive into the fundamentals of cybersecurity. Students will learn about the importance of cybersecurity, basic terminology, common threats, and essential techniques to safeguard information. A great starting point for anyone new to the field!

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand basic cybersecurity concepts and terms
  • Learn about various types of cyber threats and attacks
  • Gain knowledge of basic security measures for protecting devices and networks
  • Build a foundation for more advanced cybersecurity courses

Prerequisites

None

Duration & Schedule

  • Total Duration: 1 Month
  • Offline (Saturday & Sunday): 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (5 hours each day)
  • Online (Monday to Thursday): 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM (1 hour each day)
  • Total Course Hours: 40 hours (20 offline + 20 online)
